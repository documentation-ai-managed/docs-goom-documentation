## Citation Styles

Select your preferred style and input source details to generate formatted citations instantly.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="APA" icon="type">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter Source Details" icon="edit">
        Provide author, year, title, and publisher information.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select APA Style" icon="settings">
        Choose APA 7th edition from the dropdown.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate & Copy" icon="copy">
        Click generate, then copy the formatted citation.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <CodeGroup tabs="Input,Output">
      ```json
      {
        "author": "Smith, J.",
        "year": 2023,
        "title": "Modern Research Methods",
        "publisher": "Academic Press"
      }
      ```
      ```text
      Smith, J. (2023). *Modern research methods*. Academic Press.
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="MLA" icon="type">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Input Book Details" icon="book">
        Add author, title, container, and other edition.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose MLA 9th" icon="list">
        Select MLA from styles menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review Output" icon="eye">
        Edit if needed and export.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    ```text
    Smith, John. *Modern Research Methods*. Academic Press, 2023.
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Chicago" icon="type">
    Generate notes-bibliography or author-date formats.

    ```text
    Smith, John. 2023. *Modern Research Methods*. Chicago: Academic Press.
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Paraphrasing Workflows

Transform original text into fresh versions without altering facts. Ideal for avoiding plagiarism while retaining voice.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste Original Text" icon="clipboard">
    Input up to 500 words into the paraphraser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Mode" icon="sliders">
    Choose standard, formal, or creative paraphrase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate & Refine" icon="refresh-cw">
    Review suggestions, tweak, and integrate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CodeGroup tabs="Original,Paraphrased">
  ```text
  Climate change poses significant risks to global ecosystems.
  ```
  ```text
  Global ecosystems face substantial threats from climate change.
  ```
</CodeGroup>
