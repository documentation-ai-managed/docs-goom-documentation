## Overview

CitationGenerator.AI helps you generate citations in APA, MLA, and Chicago styles, paraphrase text, check grammar, and assess readability. You start by creating a free account, explore the dashboard, and generate your first citation in minutes. Follow these steps to enhance your academic writing today.

<Callout kind="tip">
  All tools prioritize academic integrity with plagiarism-free outputs and precise formatting.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up quickly to access all features. No credit card required for the free tier.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Site" icon="globe">
    Go to [https://citationgenerator.ai](https://citationgenerator.ai) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and verify your account via email link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="dashboard">
    Log in to reach your personalized dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Generate Your First Citation

Select a style and input source details to produce formatted citations instantly. Copy the result directly into your document.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="APA" icon="book">
    Enter book details for an APA citation.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select APA" icon="type">
        Choose **APA 7th** from the style dropdown.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Input Details">
        Source type: Book<br/>
        Author: Smith, J.<br/>
        Year: 2023<br/>
        Title: *Modern AI in Education*<br/>
        Publisher: Academic Press
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate & Copy">
        Click **Generate**. Result:

        ```text
        Smith, J. (2023). Modern AI in Education. Academic Press.
        ```
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="MLA" icon="book-open">
    Generate an MLA citation for a journal article.

    ```text
    "Article Title." Journal Name, vol. 45, no. 2, 2023, pp. 123-145.
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Chicago" icon="file-text">
    Create a Chicago-style webpage citation.

    ```text
    Last Name, First Name. "Webpage Title." Website Name. Month Day, Year. URL.
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="success">
  Citations export to Word, Google Docs, or clipboard for seamless integration.
</Callout>
