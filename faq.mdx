## Academic Integrity Guidelines

CitationGenerator.AI promotes ethical use. You always review outputs and cite sources properly to maintain academic standards.

<Callout kind="alert">
  Never submit generated content as your own work. Use tools to assist, not replace, your writing.
</Callout>

Here is an example of proper APA citation generation:

<CodeGroup tabs="APA,MLA">
  ```text
  Smith, J. (2023). *AI in Education*. Journal of Learning, 45(2), 123-145. https://doi.org/10.1234/jlearn.2023.45
  ```
  ```text
  Smith, J. "AI in Education." Journal of Learning 45.2 (2023): 123-45. Print.
  ```
</CodeGroup>
